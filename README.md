Retail Sales Insight Dashboard (Power BI)
----------------------------------------------------------

📌 Project Overview
--------------------------
This Power BI project analyzes retail sales data to provide insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, inventory risks, and marketing effectiveness. The dashboard helps management make data-driven decisions across products, customers, stores, and regions.

🏢 Business Background
---------------------------------

    🏪 Nationwide retail chain with 200+ stores across India.
   
    🌍 Operations spread across North, South, East, West, and Central regions.
   
   📦 Product categories include:

   
      ◦ 📚 Books
      ◦ 🛋️ Furniture
      ◦ 🛒 Groceries
      ◦ 👕 Clothing
      ◦ 💻 Electronics
   🌐 Sales through both physical stores and online platforms.
   📈 Focus on improving business performance through analytics and reporting.

   
🎯 Business Problem
-------------------------------------

Revenue is increasing, but profitability and performance vary across:


   ◦📦 Products
   
   ◦👥 Customers
   
   ◦🌍 Regions
   
   ◦🏪 Stores

 Management needs a dashboard to identify the factors affecting revenue, profit, and operational efficiency.

🎯 Project Goals
-------------------------------

   ◦📊 Analyze revenue and sales performance trends.
   
   ◦🏆 Identify high-performing and low-performing products and stores.
   
   ◦👥 Segment customers into categories (Regular, Silver, Gold, Platinum).
   
   ◦💰 Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
   
   ◦🔐 Enable Row-Level Security (RLS) for store managers.
   
   ◦📈 Support data-driven business decisions.


📊 Project Objectives
--------------------------------------------


   ◦💰 Monitor revenue, profit, and overall sales performance.
   
   ◦📦 Identify top-performing and underperforming products and categories.
   
   ◦🏪 Evaluate store and regional performance across different locations.
   
   ◦👥 Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior.
   
   ◦🎯 Measure the impact of discounts on profitability.
   
   ◦📉 Monitor inventory levels and identify stockout risks.
   
   ◦📈 Provide actionable insights to support business growth and decision-making.
